FAQ
What’s the difference between the three plans?

The difference is the amount of datas in your knowledgebase and the number of chat history you can view. If you choose the 16-hr plan, you can have unlimited number of data and unlimited access to view the chat history. Besides, the 16-hr plan offers you data analysis service. The difference between the 4-hr and 8-hr plan is the period of time you can use per day.

How would it be if I stop using the service?

If you unsubscribe to our service, we will keep your datas until the end of your purchasing period. After that we will delete your datas right away.

How can I benefit from Tinichat’s service?

By using our service, you can not only reduce cost to hire and train customer service agents but also get a sincere and helpful assistant to your business if you provide sufficient informations and notices to us.

How long does it take to reply our customers’ question?

We are real-time text customer service agent so if the questions is asked in the time period, we try our best to reply every messages at once. If you need our service outside of the time period, you can leave an offline message and we will get back to you as soon as possible in the next working period.

How do I start using your service? How do I coordinate with you?

First, choose a plan that suits you the best. Then, send us informations regarding your products and services such as FAQs and product informations. We will build the knowledgebase with you. If you have any other requirements, please feel free to discuss with us. The service is ready for you.

Can I get a refund if I want to unsubscribe to the service?

If you decide to unsubscribe, the service will be unavailable when the plan is due. We do not offer refund to payments that have been made.
