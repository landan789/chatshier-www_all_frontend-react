Q:

What is Text Customer Service? How’s it different from Telephone Customer Service? ( )

A:

Text Customer Service is basically like using text to communicate with customers. The difference between Text Customer Service and Telephone Customer Service is that the former could provide an environment where customers feel more comfortable to ask questions they don’t feel like asking on phone calls. Because people nowadays tend to communicate through texting, Tinichats uses the same way to provide customer service.
